Final System

We created an autonomous garden system that will water and deliver light to your plants using light and soil moisture sensors. Easily take care of your plants by just filling up a water container and then just letting the system do the work for you!

Videos of the System

These are videos of the different parts of the system working separately. We will be uploading a final video on Wednesday; stay tuned for an exciting update!

These videos show the lights responding to control, the pump responding to control, and an led showing the water level and when to refill the tank respectfully.
