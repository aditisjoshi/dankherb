GardenAI

Your in home gardener

Learn More

You can leave your plants
and still get the benefits of beauty, taste, and smell.

GardenAI is a product designed to fully automate the process of caring for plants. Using water, soil, and light sensors the model tracks metrics for each plant and provides the elements it needs to survive. Grow up to four plants in this modular device and get the benefit of delicious herbs without all of the messy work.
